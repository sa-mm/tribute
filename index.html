Rabindranath Tagore

Humanist.

Some stuff he did:

  • Published his first set of poems at age 16.
  • In addition to poetry, he wrote short stories and novels, composed songs, and painted.
  • Founded progressive schools in India and England.
  • Died in 1941 at the age of 80.
